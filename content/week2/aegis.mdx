## Overview

In this tutorial, we will set up the AEGIS system, learn how to create maps, how to move the agent, and how to save a survivor.

## Documentation 

Head over to the documention to learn everything you need: [here](https://cpsc-383.github.io/aegis/getting-started/installation/) 

## Participation

You will need to submit a screenshot showing the following:
- The killer cell placed correctly on the map. (2)
- The fire cell placed correctly on the map. (2)
- The charging cell placed correctly on the map. (2)
- The survivor placed correctly on the map. (1)
- The survivor's cell move cost is set to 5 (shown in the console). (1)
- The agent moves North then East to reach the survivor. (1)
- Your username is displayed as the group name of the agent and in the console. (1)
